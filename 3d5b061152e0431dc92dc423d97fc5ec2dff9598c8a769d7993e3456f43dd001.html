Banana Muffins

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
butter 125 g
sugar 220 g
egg 2
ripe banana 3
flour 260 g
baking soda 5 ml
salt 3 ml

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 175C (350F)
  2. Whisk the room temperatur butter in the sugar until whitened
  3. Add the eggs, bananas, flour, baking soda and salt
  4. Whisk until every ingredient is incorporated
  5. Bake 25 to 30 minutes in a muffin pan

Notes

  • Banana Bread: Bake for 55 minutes
  • Banana and nuts: Add wallnuts to the mix
