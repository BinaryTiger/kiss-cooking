Pancakes

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
flour 250 ml
salt 2 g
sugar 60 ml
milk 330 ml
egg 2
vanilla essence 10 ml
butter 30 ml

Steps

  1. Sieve the flour in a bowl
  2. Dilute the salt and sugar in milk. Incorporate the battered egg. Add vanilla essence
  3. Pour and whisk the liquid mix into the reserved flour
  4. Add the melted butter and put in the fridge for 1H

Notes

  • Citrus Waffles: Add 30 ML of zest to the mix
  • Chocolate Waffles: Replace 60 ML of flour by 125 ML of cacao
